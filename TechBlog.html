Smart Devices

The future is now and now almost every everyday object is becoming smart enabled. With a growing market of smart enabled devices there are now more ways than ever to automate your life and make your day to day tasks easier. It's becoming so common that 1 in 4 U.S. adults own a smart speaker, and to take it a step further than that every smart phone nowadays has a smart assistant built in. These things are really just the tip of the iceburg when talking about smart devices, at this day and age you can find almost every household appliance with a smart enabled version.
